Middle Layer

This layer can be solved by using two basic algorithms.

First you need to locate the edges you need to fill and

apply the algorithms to solve the Middle layer

Method 1

In this case you need to locate the edge and move it to the top of the side you want to fill

You need to use this side as your Font (F)

Then apply this method after turning the top layer downwards as given in the above picture

U R U' R' U' F' U F

Method 2

In this case you need to do the same as the previous method

You need to use this side as your Font (F)

Then apply this method after turning the top layer downwards as given in the above picture

U' L' U L U F U' F'

.

After preforming those methods for each egdes you will complete solving the second layer.
